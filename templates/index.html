FAQ:

Q: Why does this look kinda terrible?

A: Assuming that you are using this website for its purpose, the main value of the project is retreival of information. There is no need to present it in a presentable manner since usually you are comparing two champions during discussions such as VOD review or you were thinking of a matchup and wanted to quickly just glance at range comparisons. (bro im lazy ok?)

Q: Why is there range, target range, effect radius, and collision radius for every spell?? What do those even mean??

A: Since this project is parsing through the wiki (leagueoflegends.fandom.com) to obtain the information instead of asking Data Dragon, we must use the values given by the wiki. For each ability in the wiki, there are 4 different parameters that indicate range. Range, target range, effect radius, and collision radius. You can usually just think of them as interchangable.
